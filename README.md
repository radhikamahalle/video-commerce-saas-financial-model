# Video Commerce SaaS Financial Model

## Overview

This repository showcases a Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) model developed for an early-stage B2B SaaS startup operating in the video commerce industry.

The objective of this project was to build a dynamic financial model that supports budgeting, forecasting, fundraising, and strategic business planning.

> **Note:** All confidential company information has been removed or anonymized.

---

# Business Overview

The business operates as a subscription-based SaaS platform that enables brands to connect with customers through live video consultations. The platform helps improve customer engagement, build trust, and increase online sales conversions.

---

# Business Problem

The company wanted to:

- Forecast future revenue
- Estimate operating expenses
- Understand funding requirements
- Monitor monthly cash flow
- Plan hiring
- Support investor fundraising discussions
